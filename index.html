Welcome to the battle of the 2 football Titans!

  • The aim of the game is to gain a high weapon damage score and attack the other player.

  • If players move 3 tiles they gain life, if they move from 1 to 2 tiles than they lose life.

  • Once the players meet, a fight begins.
